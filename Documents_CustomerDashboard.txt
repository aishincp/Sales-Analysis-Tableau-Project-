CUSTOMER DASHBOARD

Dashboard Purpose:
Aims to provide an overview of customer data, trends and behaviors. It will help marketing teams & management to understand customer segments & improve customer satisfaction.

Key Requirements:
1. KPI Overview:
Display a summary of total no of customers, total sales per customer & total number of orders for current and previous year

2. Customer Trends:
- Present the data for each KPI on a monthly basis for both current year & previous year
- Identify months with highest & lowest sales & make them easy to recognize

3. Customer Distribution by Number of Orders:
- Represent the distribution of customers based on the number of orders they have placed to provide insights into customer behavior, loyalty and engagement.

4. Top 10 Customers by Profit:
- Present top 10 customers who have generated highest profits for company
- Show additional information like rank, number of orders, current sales, current profit & last order date.
